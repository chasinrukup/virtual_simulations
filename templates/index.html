Step 1 — Define Subnets
A subnet is an isolated network segment. Machines in the same subnet talk freely. Machines in different subnets are isolated — they need a firewall to communicate.

Tip: Start with 2 subnets (e.g. WAN + LAN). You can add up to 5.
Quick add:
Step 2 — Add Virtual Machines
Place VMs on your subnets. Use endpoint for regular machines (servers, attackers). Use firewall to connect subnets — it bridges a WAN side to one or more LAN sides.
